Active Recall

Active Recall Checklist

Create a set of questions from each chapter.
Attempt answers without looking at notes.
Mark questions you struggled with for review.


Spaced Repetition

Spaced Repetition Steps

Initial review immediately after learning.
Second review after 1 day.
Next review after 3 days.
Subsequent review after 1 week, then 1 month.


Note-Taking Styles

Note-Taking Methods

Cornell Method

Divide page into cues, notes, and summary sections.

Structure:
Cues | Notes | Summary
Best For:
Review and self-testing

Outline Method

Organize points using headings and subpoints.

Structure:
Hierarchical headings and subpoints
Best For:
Organizing detailed lectures
